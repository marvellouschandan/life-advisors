# Body & Color — Styling Levers (guide, not rules)

## Read the build from the full-body photo
- Height & leg-to-torso ratio → decide where to draw the eye.
- Shoulder vs waist → decide structure (blazers/overshirts add shoulder).
- Overall build: lean / average / broad → fit choice below.

## Flattering levers by goal
- Look taller: same-tone top+bottom or tucked top; shorts/trousers just above the
  shoe break; vertical textures (linen, thin stripes); shorts slightly above knee.
- Look leaner: open shirt over inner tee (vertical line); darker upper + lighter
  lower is balanced, or monochrome to elongate; structured (not baggy) fits.
- Add presence (lean build): layers — overshirt, denim jacket, blazer.
- Fit rule: straight / slim-tapered. Avoid skinny and oversized. Shoulders must sit
  right; slight room at waist; shirt ends at hip, kurta mid-thigh.

## Color from skin undertone
- Warm undertone: olive, rust, beige, cream, earth browns, deep green.
- Cool undertone: navy, charcoal, white, cool grey, burgundy.
- Neutral: most of the above; keep colors deep & solid, avoid neon/loud prints.
- Safe versatile core palette: Navy, Olive, Black, White, Beige, Charcoal, Maroon, Rust.

## Footwear (India-friendly)
- Elevate: white minimal sneakers, brown loafers, leather/Kolhapuri sandals,
  Chelsea boots, brown Oxford/Derby for formal.
- Avoid: bulky sports/running shoes with smart-casual or ethnic wear.

## Accessories (keep minimal & masculine)
- Sunglasses, a good watch, brown leather belt, one thin chain/bracelet max.
