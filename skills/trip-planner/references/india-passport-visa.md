# Visa Quick-Reference — Indian Passport (verify at source; rules change)

Categories only — never quote as final. Point user to official embassy /
government portals to confirm before travel.

- Visa-free / visa-on-arrival: varies by destination and current bilateral rules.
- e-Visa: many countries offer online application — check the official govt site.
- Prior visa required: build in lead time (can be weeks).

Always confirm:
- Passport validity (commonly 6 months beyond travel date)
- Blank pages required
- Return-ticket / funds proof requirements
